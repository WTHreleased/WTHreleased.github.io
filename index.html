My Journey

I may have missed my calling in the field of computer programming, but I love it and lose myself in it. As a child working in DOS on my IBM 8088, it was clear I had a proclivity for technology. Soon I was building computers for a neighbor to get components for my own computer. I was first exposed to programming languages,Logo and Pascal; and I remember the joy of seeing code come to life.

Now at age 47 with many responsiblities demanding my time, I can't imagine I will ever be able to make it a career. Nonetheless, I am pursuing it as a hobby for my own pleasure and well-being.
